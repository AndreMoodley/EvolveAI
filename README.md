Understanding this philosophy is not optional context. It is the operating system the entire app is built on.

#### Phase VII — The Biomechanics of the Origin Art

To ensure neuroplastic adaptations are flawless, the biomechanical parameters of the movement must never deviate into Negative Cultivation. **Andre's Muscle-Up Technique** represents the perfected, individualized execution of the strict bar muscle-up — the synthesis of kinesiological principles designed to maximize leverage while protecting the joints from shearing forces.

The technique requires strict adherence to four fundamental biomechanical rules:

1. **The Hang and False Grip** — Dead hang with a deeply set false grip. Wrists positioned over the bar, meat of the palm resting on the apex of the cylinder. Shortens the lever arm. Eliminates grip adjustment during the critical transition phase.

2. **The Scapular and Bent-Arm Pull** — Initiating with aggressive scapular retraction and depression, securing the shoulder girdle. Followed immediately by a powerful, explosive lat contraction pulling the bar cleanly to the lower chest, elbows tucked tightly toward the midline.

3. **The Seamless Transition (The Crucible)** — The crux of the movement and the primary failure point for the Half-Awakened. The practitioner aggressively shifts the hips backward while simultaneously driving the head and shoulders completely over the bar — using the torque generated by the false grip to seamlessly pivot the body's center of mass. No kipping. No elevator fault. A pivot.

4. **The Strict Dip and Controlled Descent** — Finishing with a strict, locked-out triceps extension into a high support hold. The descent must reverse the pathway exactly — controlled, false grip maintained, structural tension preserved — arriving at the dead hang ready for consecutive, momentum-free repetitions.

### The Void Entity

The practitioner's realm, ki state, and shadow level do not exist only as numbers. They are made visible.

The Void Entity is an animated humanoid silhouette rendered at the top of the Today screen. It is not decorative. It is a live read of the practitioner's current state — the closest thing the system has to a mirror.

**What it reflects:**

**Realm (1–7)** drives the entity's fundamental form. The body grows taller as strikes accumulate. Aura rings expand outward. Orbit particles emerge from zero at Foundation to six at Divine Master. At realm 1 the figure is dim, barely manifested. At realm 7 it is fully opaque, imposing, and surrounded by orbiting energy. The change is not cosmetic — the entity at realm 1 is a fundamentally different object than the entity at realm 7, because the practitioner is.

**Ki (0–100)** controls the eyes. Above 60 they burn with the practitioner's chosen aura colour. Between 30–60 they shift to gold. Below 30 they drop to red. The eyes pulse continuously; their rhythm doesn't change, but what they signal does.

**Shadow level (1–5)** controls stance. At level 1 the arms are drawn close. At level 5 they spring outward — the wide, aggressive posture of a released shadow. The arm spread animates with a spring curve whenever shadow level changes, making the entity physically respond to the practitioner's internal state.

**Streak (≥ 7 days)** adds one bonus orbit particle beyond the realm-standard count.

**Aura signature**

From the Self tab → *Aura Colour*, the practitioner selects the entity's energy signature. This is the one customisable element — everything else evolves automatically.

| Key | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| `ki` | Ki | `#7cf6ff` cyan |
| `gold` | Power | `#f5b301` gold |
| `crimson` | Void | `#c91538` crimson |
| `white` | Pure | `#dde8ff` white |

Selection persists in `AsyncStorage` (`@evolveai/character/v1`) and is shared across the app via `CharacterProvider`.

**Implementation**

Built entirely with React Native's `Animated` API — no SVG, no external animation library. All animations that drive `transform` and `opacity` run on the native thread (`useNativeDriver: true`). The character is composed of absolutely-positioned `View` elements on a 280 × 268 canvas: head, two glowing eyes, neck, torso, two arms, two legs. Orbit particles use rotating container views with a fixed-offset child to trace circular paths without trigonometry. Three aura rings breathe at different frequencies, creating a naturally asynchronous pulse. Arm spread is spring-animated whenever shadow level changes.

Relevant files: `components/Character/SpiritEntity.js`, `store/character-context.js`.

Traditional cognitive science models the mind using classical probability theory — beliefs and outcomes exist as definitive, predetermined coordinates. **Temporal Architecture**

The perception of time is not a fixed chronological constant. It is a highly malleable psychological construct dependent on attentional density, emotional valence, and the speed of cognitive processing.

The **Inverse Consciousness-Time Theory** posits that subjective experience of time is inversely proportional to the intensity of conscious awareness. As the practitioner elevates awareness and increases the velocity of thought, time intrinsically slows from their subjective frame of reference. Bending time is not mystical. It is the direct, measurable consequence of optimizing neuro-cognitive velocity.

To maximize temporal harvest, the practitioner structures their day into three distinct sessions — partitioning a standard 24-hour cycle into three lived "days." This is aggressive **XP farming**, granting years of experiential learning in weeks. Critically, this is a *cognitive* partitioning of waking hours — not a reduction in sleep. Total sleep must remain consolidated and monophasic to preserve the neuroplasticity required for constant transformation.

### Metacognitive Decentering & Shadow Integration

**Detached Mindfulness**

Psychological mastery requires a state of profound depersonalization — **metacognitive decentering** — allowing the practitioner to act as the objective observer of their own internal landscape.

They are fundamentally separate from the core self. When a severe negative emotion or worldly desire arises, the practitioner applies *low conceptual processing* — they do not analyze the thought, they do not suppress it, they do not engage in goal-directed coping. They observe it as raw data being presented to their central executive function.

This successfully interrupts the **Cognitive Attentional Syndrome (CAS)** — the destructive, energy-consuming cycle of rumination, constant worrying, and hyper-vigilant threat monitoring that paralyzes execution. Emotions become *opinions* offered by the lower, primitive brain. Heard. Registered. Never acted upon.

This is the activation of the **Spirit Eye** — the fourth-dimensional vantage point that sees the world exactly as it is, stripped of emotional distortion and the suffocating weight of worldly attachments.

True psychological mastery does not require the eradication of dark, aggressive, or intense emotions. Attempting to completely sanitize the psyche results in catastrophic loss of drive.

Within every high-performer lies an innate, primal hunger — the **Jungian Shadow**: the unconscious, deeply repressed aspects of the personality housing unbridled aggression, overwhelming ego, impatience, and the capacity for ruthless execution. Many suppress these traits due to societal conditioning, labeling natural aggression as dysfunctional. This repression drains massive psychological energy and blunts the competitive edge.

The goal is not to destroy the monster. It is to achieve **complete shadow integration** — recognizing the raw, nuclear power of these repressed traits and consciously channeling them into the training environment during moments of extreme physical exertion. The shadow becomes a tool: a reservoir of emotional intensity that dictates the magnitude of physical change, not a master that manipulates holistic reality.

### Restorative Protocols

Maintaining relentless output demands recovery protocols that are equally extreme and precise. Standard passive rest is insufficient for an organism operating at the bleeding edge of capability.

NSDR guides the brain into a liminal state at the precise boundary between wakefulness and sleep. This clears metabolic waste from the brain, regulates the HPA axis diurnal cortisol pattern, and massively accelerates neuroplasticity — locking in the neural adaptations required for advanced motor patterns.

## Quick Start

### 1 — Backend

```bash
cd server
cp .env.example .env
# Edit .env — set DATABASE_URL, JWT_SECRET, ANTHROPIC_API_KEY

npm install
npm run db:migrate        # Runs all Prisma migrations; creates tables
npm run db:seed           # Optional: seeds demo practitioner + sample vows
npm run dev               # Starts server with nodemon on http://localhost:4000
```

Confirm the server is healthy:

```bash
curl http://localhost:4000/health
# → { "status": "ok" }
```

No local PostgreSQL? Use Docker:

```bash
docker run --name evolveai-db \
  -e POSTGRES_USER=evolveai \
  -e POSTGRES_PASSWORD=secret \
  -e POSTGRES_DB=evolveai \
  -p 5432:5432 \
  -d postgres:15
```

Then set `DATABASE_URL="postgresql://evolveai:secret@localhost:5432/evolveai"` in `server/.env`. See [`server/README.md`](server/README.md) for a full Docker Compose setup.

### 2 — Mobile Client

```bash
npm install
npx expo start
```

- Press **`i`** → iOS Simulator (requires Xcode on macOS)
- Press **`a`** → Android Emulator (requires Android Studio)
- Scan the QR code with **Expo Go** for a physical device

## Running on a Physical Device

`localhost` does not resolve to your dev machine from a phone. Find your LAN IP:

```bash
# macOS
ipconfig getifaddr en0

# Linux
ip route get 1 | awk '{print $7; exit}'

# Windows
ipconfig | findstr "IPv4"
```

Then start Expo with the override:

```bash
EXPO_PUBLIC_API_URL=http://192.168.1.50:4000 npx expo start
```

Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network. If requests still fail, confirm your firewall allows inbound connections on port 4000.

## Architecture Notes

**Auth**
- JWT bearer tokens (HS256), signed with `JWT_SECRET`
- Stored in `AsyncStorage` on the client
- API client in `util/api.js` checks expiry on every request and silently refreshes when ≤ 1 minute remains
- All protected routes require `Authorization: Bearer <token>`; `requireAuth` middleware rejects without a valid token with `401`

**Persistence Model**
- Client writes locally to `AsyncStorage` first (optimistic update), then syncs to the backend
- On login / app resume, the backend is the **source of truth** and overwrites: `hammerCount`, `ki`, `shadowLevel`, `leaks`
- UI preferences and unsent session drafts are not overwritten
- Failed syncs are queued and retried on the next successful connection

**Realm Computation**
- Realms are computed client-side from cumulative `hammerCount` using breakpoints in `constants/realms.js`
- No realm data is stored in the database — only `hammerCount` persists
- Realm thresholds can be adjusted without a migration

**Validation**
- All incoming request bodies are validated by `zod` schemas defined alongside each route
- Validation errors return `400` with a structured error array: `{ errors: [{ field, message }] }`

**AI Coach**
- `POST /api/ai/coach` forwards the practitioner's current realm, active vows, Ki level, and streak data to the Anthropic API as context
- The system prompt is seeded entirely from the Void Protocol source texts and the four-pillar framework
- Responses are streamed to the client where supported

## Troubleshooting

**`npm run db:migrate` fails with "connection refused"**
PostgreSQL is not running or `DATABASE_URL` is incorrect. Verify host, port, username, and password. If using Docker, confirm the container is up: `docker ps`.

**Expo shows "Network request failed" on device**
You are using `localhost` on a physical phone. Follow the [Running on a Physical Device](#running-on-a-physical-device) section and set `EXPO_PUBLIC_API_URL` to your LAN IP.

**`invalid signature` / `jwt malformed` errors**
The token in `AsyncStorage` may be stale from a previous `JWT_SECRET`. Clear app storage or log out and log back in.

**AI coach returns empty responses**
Confirm `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` is set in `server/.env` and is valid. Check server logs for a `401` from the Anthropic API.

**Realm not updating after strikes**
